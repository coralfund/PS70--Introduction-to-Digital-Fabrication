I was touched by a film I saw Blue Communique, which inspired my to propose using the owl in the science project. The feeling of Blue Comminuque which I had hoped to encapsulate was one that creates meaning with technology- and lends a mythology to something as mechanical as a motor or drone. Chase wrote most of the code for the project and I had to miss that class due to sickness but I am assured the machine had a deeper dimension.
