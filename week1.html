<h3>Introduction and Documentation Week </h3> <h4> Four Project Examples</h4> <p> Originally, I came up with 4 other ideas to work on besides the POV, which are as follows: A. Tooth Mounted Chip to track diet- This ideas was inspired by a Tufts research article that used a combination of silk and gold to track the quality of the food. I eversince wondered what this device would be like if it could give continuous data on diet. After much research, I had to abandon this project because it was more biochemistry heavy but I learned a lot about silicone minituarization, non-invasive biotracking and nutrition. B. IoT Ring- An iot ring that would connect to all devices and eventually a laser based headset. C. Matcha Machine- A Keurig for matcha. D. Perfume Burner- Inspired by the 1960 3 foot solid silver perfume burner designed by Phillip Rolles for the Chatsworth Estate. E. LUI (Laser User Interface)- Designing an AR heatset that used a system of lasers to form images, as opposed to GUI interfaces. Ultimately, I found it a more suitable subject to focus on building a Laser Persistence of Vision, while aligned more with the themes of the class.
