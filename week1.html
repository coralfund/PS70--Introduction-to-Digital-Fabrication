Weekly Assignment 1: Create a press-fit construction kit that can be assembled in multiple ways, and accounts for lasercutter kerf. Work through a Fusion 360 tutorial and document what you produce. Select at least 2 household objects or components from the lab. Measure them using calipers, and model them in Fusion 360 (or other program). Create an assembly using these components, and document your work on your webpage. You may also incorporate items from other 3D model repositories like GrabCad.
